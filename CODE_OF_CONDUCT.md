# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The Albion Data Client project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior and the consequences for unacceptable conduct.

## Expected Behavior

All participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful and inclusive of all individuals
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be professional and courteous in all communications
- Focus on constructive criticism and collaboration
- Respect differing opinions and experiences
- Report concerns through appropriate channels

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any individual
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, ethnicity, or similar personal characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Threats of violence or violent language
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Posting others' private information without consent
- Spamming or trolling
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed, investigated, and appropriate action will be taken. Project maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories and issue trackers
- Project communications and discussions
- Project events and related activities
- Official project spaces
